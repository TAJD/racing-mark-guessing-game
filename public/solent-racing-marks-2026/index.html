Each year the Solent Cruising and Racing Association (SCRA) publishes the definitive list of racing marks for the Solent. The data is collated and distributed by Winning Tides and forms the basis for most club racing in the area.

For the 2026 season there are 157 marks, down from 161 in 2025. The net reduction comes from four marks being removed with none added. Beyond the removals, two marks shifted position by more than 250 metres — meaningful for course-setting and tactical planning — and 23 marks changed name, mostly reflecting new or departed sponsors.

This page breaks down every change, starting with the positional moves that matter most to racers.

Marks that moved

A mark moving more than 50 m can shift a rounding gate, change the lay-line, or require a course amendment notice. Both changes below are significant at race pace.
